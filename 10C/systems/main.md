## 6.3 Elimination

Multiply equations to get equal and opposite coefficients, then add.

$$\begin{cases} 2x + 3y = 7 \\ 4x - 3y = 5 \end{cases} \xrightarrow{+} 6x = 12 \implies x = 2$$
